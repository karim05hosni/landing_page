Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions

About the Platform

What is Bunyan?

Bunyan is a cloud-based software platform for course management and academic assessment, providing tools for managing attendance, grades, assignments, and exams.

Who is this platform for?

Designed for instructors and lecturers in training centers and educational institutions who need simple, effective tools for managing their courses.

Do I need to install software?

No, Bunyan is a cloud platform that works entirely through your web browser. Access it from any internet-connected device.

Subscription & Payment

How do I subscribe?

Select your preferred plan from the pricing page. All payments are securely processed through our authorized payment provider.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard) and other electronic payment methods through our payment provider.

Can I change my plan?

Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime from your dashboard. Cost differences are prorated.

What happens when my subscription ends?

Your account becomes read-only. You can export your data but cannot add new data until you renew.

Usage

How do students record attendance?

Students scan a QR code displayed during class. Instructors can also record attendance manually.

Can students access the platform?

Yes, there's a dedicated student interface for submitting assignments, taking exams, and viewing grades (after publication).

Can I export my data?

Yes, export all your data in Excel format anytime. PDF reports are also available for printing.

Support

How do I contact support?

Email us or use the contact form on our Contact page. We typically respond within one business day.
