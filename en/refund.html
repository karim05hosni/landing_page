Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

We strive for your complete satisfaction with Bunyan services. This policy outlines refund and cancellation terms.

Satisfaction Guarantee

We offer a 14-day satisfaction guarantee from your first subscription date. If unsatisfied during this period, request a full refund with no questions asked.

Cancellation

  • Cancel anytime from your dashboard
  • Account remains active until current billing period ends
  • No additional charges after cancellation
  • Export your data before subscription ends

Requesting a Refund

  1. Contact us via Contact page
  2. State your refund reason
  3. We respond within 3-5 business days
  4. If approved, refund processed within 5-10 business days

Refund Eligibility

Eligible:

  • Request within 14 days of first subscription
  • Extended service unavailability due to our technical issues
  • Billing errors or incorrect charges

Not eligible:

  • Request after 14 days (except exceptional cases)
  • Terms of service violations
  • Change of mind after full service use

Refund Method

Refunds are issued via the original payment method through our payment provider (Paddle). Processing takes 5-10 business days depending on your bank.
