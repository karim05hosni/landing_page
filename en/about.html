About Us

Building reliable software tools for educational institutions

Our Vision

Bunyan is a cloud-based academic management and assessment platform designed for training centers and educational institutions. We believe technology should simplify academic work, not complicate it.

The Team

Founded by software engineers specializing in building reliable and user-friendly academic systems. We focus on delivering practical technical solutions that meet the needs of educational institutions.

Our Mission

We aim to provide an integrated system that enables instructors to focus on teaching rather than administrative tasks, through simple and effective tools for managing attendance, grades, assignments, and exams.

Location

We operate from Saudi Arabia and serve educational institutions across the region.
