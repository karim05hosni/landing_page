Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

At Bunyan, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account data: Name, email, phone number
  • Usage data: Login logs, pages visited, actions taken
  • Content data: Courses, students, grades, assignments you enter
  • Payment data: Processed via our authorized payment provider

3. How We Use Data

  • Providing and operating platform services
  • Processing payments and subscriptions
  • Sending important service notifications
  • Improving platform and user experience
  • Technical support and customer service

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Service providers: Such as Paddle for payment processing
  • Infrastructure providers: Cloud hosting services
  • Legal requirements: When necessary for legal compliance

5. Data Security

  • Data encrypted in transit and at rest (SSL/TLS, AES-256)
  • Role-based access control
  • Regular backups
  • Secure server hosting

6. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion
  • Export your data
  • Object to certain processing

7. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact us via Contact page.
